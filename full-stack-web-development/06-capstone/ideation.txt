1. Can you summarize in two sentences the central idea of your project?

- MyMovies is a website for keeping track of movies you've watched, want to
  watch and don't want to watch. It leverages the IMDB database by allowing the
  user to Add custom information like rate and comments.

2. Do you have any examples that you look up to for inspiration to suggest
   ideas for your project?

- filmow.com already implements a similar idea.

3. How are the end-users going to benefit from your idea?

- Cinema fans.

4. What is the opportunity gap that you are trying to fill with your idea?

- Tracking the movie watching habits of the user will help the cinema industry
  on targeting its customer more precisely.
